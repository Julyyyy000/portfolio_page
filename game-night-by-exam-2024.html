Overview

Role: self-developed — programming / art / game design

Night By Exam (2024) is a 2D pixel-art narrative puzzle game about exam anxiety, intrusive thoughts, and the emotional pressure surrounding academic performance. Developed in Unity 2D for the 2024 Mental Health Champion event. Below is the demo video:

Design & Themes

The player is stuck in looping exam-night scenarios, navigating spaces that mirror cluttered thoughts and spiraling self-criticism. Puzzle mechanics echo cognitive distortions: you revisit scenes, try different approaches, and confront the feeling of never being “prepared enough.”

Tools & Contributions

  • Engine: Unity 2D
  • Art: original pixel sprites and UI
  • Programming: core gameplay systems, puzzle logic, scene flow
  • Design: narrative structure, pacing, environmental storytelling
